King Saul was the first king of Israel.
One day, the Philistines came to fight.
Their giant, Goliath, was big and scary; and no one wanted to face him.

Then young David came to the camp, and David loved God and trusted Him.
David said, “God is with me.”
He picked up his sling and five smooth stones from the stream.

David swung his sling and one stone flew through the air.
It hit Goliath on the head and the giant fell down.
David had won, and everyone cheered.

Everyone loved David after that day.
He became the next king of Israel.
