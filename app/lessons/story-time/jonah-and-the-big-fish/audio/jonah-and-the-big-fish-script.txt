God told Jonah to go to the city of Nineveh, but Jonah did not want to.
He got on a boat and sailed away.

While he was at sea, God sent a big storm.
The wind blew hard and the waves crashed against the boat.
The sailors were scared but Jonah was asleep!
The sailors ran to Jonah and shouted, “Wake up!”

Jonah told the sailors it was his fault.
The sailors threw Jonah into the sea and the storm became calm.
Then God sent a big fish.
The fish swallowed Jonah, GULP! BURP!

Inside the fish, Jonah prayed to God for three days.
“I’m sorry. I will go to Nineveh.”
God forgave Jonah so the fish went to Ninevah and spat out Jonah.
Jonah told the people God’s message; and the people said sorry and changed.
